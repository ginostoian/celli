Small bathroom renovation

Even a small bathroom can make a big difference if done properly. Our designers have a passion for taking small bathrooms and making them an oasis of relaxation. Combining functionality and practicality with great design and clever storage solutions we manage to get rid of the chaos that usually comes with a tiny bathroom.

Contemporary bathroom renovation

A delicate balance between old materials used in a new way with cutting edge appliances sets apart a contempary bathroom from a traditional one. Even if you don't opt for a freestanding bath, you can still get the stylish contemporary look by using stone tiles and porcelain basins with sleek lines and simle contrasting colors.

Modern bathroom renovation

Remodelling a bathroom in a modern style should focus on some key concepts. Geometrical shapes, simple color palettes with sleek bold accents or tones of neutral colors highlighted by minimal decorative elements is the way to go. A wet room is also distinct landmark used to create an open plan in the modern theme.

Industrial bathroom renovation

Even if you opt for a strong industrial style or you want a soft version of it, you can use metro tiles, grey colors, bare brick walls, exposed shelving and black shower frames. These are the distinctive elements that define the original industrial style to which our designers will add their particular aesthetic skills.

Rustic bathroom renovation

Don't want to leave the big city but love the feel of a farmhouse? A refined rustic bathroom might be exactly what you need. Combining the natural warm look of rough stone walls and exposed wooden beams, with timeless elegant designs, enables you to keep a modern look without losing your home's history.

Victorian bathroom renovation

This bathroom from Victoria Plum is the perfect example of the traditional Victorian look. This design style often features a freestanding bath as the center piece with ornate basins or wash stands and brassware.
