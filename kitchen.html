What kind of kitchen design are you after?

The first step when you are thinking about renovating or remodelling is to establish your intentions. We know that making this decision is not an easy task, so below you can find different kitchen styles that can help you make the best decision when you pick a design style.

Whether you choose traditional or modern design, we can make your kitchen suit your needs and taste.

Small kitchen renovation

No home is complete without a great kitchen. A small kitchen, with careful planning, a little imagination and a professional building team can become functional and practical instead of being a stresful to use space. Our designers have a passion for taking small kitchens and making them a chef's heaven. Combining functionality and practicality with great design and clever storage solutions we manage to get rid of the chaos that usually comes with a tiny kitchen.

Contemporary kitchen renovation

A delicate balance between old materials used in a new way with cutting edge appliances sets apart a contempary kitchen from a traditional one. Even if you don't opt for a kitchen island, you can still get the stylish contemporary look by using contemporary design elements and color schemes that create a distinctive look which transcends trends.

Modern kitchen renovation

A modern kitchen is clever because it stays away from busy design and ornamentation. This makes it one of the most popular choices amongst our clients. Neutral beautiful Remodelling a bathroom in a modern style should focus on some key concepts. Geometrical shapes, simple color palettes with sleek bold accents or tones of neutral colors highlighted by minimal decorative elements is the way to go. A wet room is also distinct landmark used to create an open plan in the modern theme.

Minimalist kitchen renovation

The minimalist style is defined by an airy ambiance which you can get by using high quality materials with clean lines, simplicity, elegant aesthetics and a neutral color pallete. When considering your minimalist kitchen layout, you should have in mind innovative storage and multi functional funrniture that fits your lifestyle requirements. Avoiding complicated and fussy designs is smartest way to make your minimalist kitchen look its best.

Open plan kitchen renovation

A popular choice nowadays is to knock down a wall between your kitchen and dining room creating an open plan kitchen. This can transform a cramped dark room in a big, open and enjoyable place to entertain and amaze your family and friends.

Kitchen extension renovation

Your extension project is done and you don't know what to use the space for. How about moving the hub of the house in here? Kitchens nowadays are not just for cooking. Having a kitchen in an extension enables the space to be a place for living, dining, socializing and even doing homework with your little ones.
