Top 10 Bathroom Design Trend of 2022

Are you trying to find new ideas for your bathroom? Specialists have predicted bathroom design trends for 2022. Before beginning a project in 2022, it's a good idea to look at what will be popular in bathrooms next year to make sure your design remains current. In recent years, trends for bathrooms have evolved to include a wide range of different designs and colours that were previously reserved for more utilitarian spaces, allowing you to express yourself more in your design than ever before. We enlisted the help of some of the best bathroom designers in the business to give us a heads-up on some of the hottest design ideas for 2022. Here are 10 of our favourites.

Should I Need to Follow Bathroom Trends?

Bathroom trends don't often go out of style as fast as those in other home areas. Consequently, it's a good idea to keep this in mind while evaluating whether or not to commit to a trend. If you're going to make a selection based on your preferences, you'll have to consider current trends. Grey grout and White metro tiles, considered standard in bathroom decor, were formerly outmoded; yet, some fads are more durable than others.

How Do Bathroom Renovation and Refurbishment Work?

When it comes to bathroom remodelling, you want to make sure that you do everything you can to protect your property and the environment. If you’re not taking steps such as protecting the flooring, then there is a high chance that your bathroom will become a mess. Bathroom renovation and refurbishment can be a great way to help protect both of these factors. This type of renovation can also save you money on your remodelling project. In addition, bathroom renovation and refurbishment can help improve the appearance of your bathroom.

Bathroom renovation: This is a popular trend in the bathroom industry, as people are looking for ways to update their bathrooms without scrapping everything. Many people are interested in renovating their bathrooms for a few reasons: to save on money, make them more comfortable, or improve the appearance of the room.

Bathroom refurbishment: This is a similar style of renovation, but it's used for updating the look and function of an existing bathroom. Often, this involves adding new features or changing the bathroom's layout to be more comfortable and organized.

Bathroom contractor – These days, there are a lot of companies that offer bathroom renovation though you should always do your research before hiring a bathroom refurbishment specialist.

Green in Bathroom

Large colour forecasting companies such as Dulux and Pantone make forecasts every year about the hues that will characterize the following year. However, the reality may be considerably different from projections. In contrast to Dulux's Blue Skies and Pantone's Very Peri, searches for sage and forest green have increased by 23% in the last year. It's a good idea to use green in the bathroom, particularly in these warmer shades. The colour contrasts nicely with brass and copper accents and lends a sense of cosiness to the bathroom as a whole.

Open Showers

Is the standalone tub's heyday over in 2022? Bathroom merchants say there's a growing preference for a particular sort of shower. In addition to moving away from smaller cubicles and toward considerably larger enclosures, more consumers opt for walk-in showers and wet rooms over bathtubs, according to Sanctuary Bathrooms' Showroom Supervisor and 3D Bathroom Designer.

Use wet rooms and floors to make the most of your available space while also making your house more adaptable in the event of ageing family members or guests.

Stone Finishes Statement

It is fashionable to use large swaths of marble or other stone, yet doing so may be prohibitively costly even for those with the most significant budgets. According to a tile industry expert, "from marble-effect to onyx effect, stone effect designs are making their mark in the world of tiles this season". "Stone effect tiles are less expensive than real stone because they are typically made of porcelain or ceramic.

Creative Lighting of Bathroom

Though elaborate and intriguing bathroom lighting concepts have been around for a while, in 2022, you can hope to see even more of them on the market. If you're planning to utilize these new designs in your bathroom, you'll need a higher IP rating because of the elevated risk of electrocution. For your bathroom remodel in 2022, one of your top priorities should be to include a variety of lighting kinds and styles to create a more complex lighting system.

Ceramics Coloured for Basins and Baths

It's hard to imagine avocado green or whisper pink, but we're already seeing a lot of interest in brightly coloured porcelain sinks and toilets, which were popular in the 1980s and 1990s. More and more people are opting for brighter and more colourful bathroom decor as an alternative to the traditional antiseptic white boxes. However, will these bathroom designs be able to withstand the test of time?

Choose a more substantial statement piece instead of the whole suite of colourful ceramics if you're worried about the bathroom's lifespan. If you're wary of statement basins in the future, you can quickly and cheaply change them. They provide a lot of impact without taking up too much room.

Biophilic Design and Wellbeing

For physical and psychological reasons, health has taken on critical importance in the home. This isn't much of a surprise, given recent occurrences. As more people worked from home or educated their children at home, there was an uptick in the number of homeowners and designers building places that served as respites. As more individuals work from home, the need for a peaceful haven in the house hasn't lessened, thanks to a newfound appreciation for the simple things.

In the bathroom, biophilic design means designing a space designed to stimulate health benefits. This might involve adapting natural materials like water and mud into the home facade or bathroom décor.

Spa Bathrooms and Steam Rooms

No one can deny the popularity of treating one's house like a spa day after day. It's like having your spa in your own home, with features like built-in Bluetooth speakers, heated seats, and luxurious finishes. This way, you can take the idea of "being able to relax anywhere" and make it available at your fingertips.

Fluted Features

As designers and businesses introduce new fluted goods, you may expect to see them more often in bathroom design by 2022. Fluted designs have been buzzing about interior design for the last year.

Tiles and New fluted basins are a huge trend for 2022, even though fluted bathroom furniture is readily available. Wall panelling ideas with fluted finishes may also be used in a modern bathroom.

Terracotta Bathrooms

Blues and greens are traditional choices for smooth bathroom colour schemes, but a variety of earthy tones are quickly gaining popularity as well. Terracotta is one of the most popular colours and materials for the bathroom right now, along with taupes, beiges, and browns.

Terracotta, a new colour trend for 2022, has a calming effect on the house, like green. Cement tiles may be used in the bathroom, as can towels and bathmats in a warm apricot tone to mimic the look and feel of a Mediterranean home.

Period Styles with Mixing Modern

The popularity of traditional bathroom designs continues to grow, but there are methods to get a classic aesthetic without it becoming cheesy. One way to do it right is to give a traditional bathroom suite a modern touch with colour choices while also honouring its origins.

This style is all about how well you can merge the two types. Consumers can express themselves in a truly unique way for the first time due to their bathroom design. Making it work is all about the ingredients you use. Take a moment to remember that opposites attract! In this traditional bathroom, the faucet has been updated to be more contemporary and streamlined in appearance—classic faucets on a cutting-edge sink design.
